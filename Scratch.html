How Winston Became a Woodbury ... in steps

  1. Mom and Dad confirmed with Abigail that getting a dog would be okay.
    • Abigail was terrified of dogs so it was crazy that she agreed.
  2. Dad drove all night.
    • Winston was a Christmas suprise, so Dad drove in the middle of the night to get him so none of us would know.
  3. Christmas morning excitement.
    • When Christmas morning finally arrived the four sisters waited at the top of the stairs. Dad was confirming that Santa had come and Mom was grabbing the camera. Lauren turned to Abigail and said, "Do you hear that? I think it is a dog." Abigail told her sister that she was wrong, hoping that Lauren had not figured out the suprise.

    • After taking their Christmas picture on the stairs the girls ran into the family room and found the cutest puppy
